Kahurangi Expedition 2023

In November 2023 Louie Burger, Leo Easton and Luka Johnson set off from a road bend near Murcherison ( the southernmost point of the Kahurangi National Park. ) They were headed for the northernmost point ( Farewell Spit ) with the goal of staying in the National Park the entire time.
